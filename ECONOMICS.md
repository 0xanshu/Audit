# Unit Economics & Path to $1M ARR

## The Business Model

Audit is a lead generation tool for a consulting or credits service. The free audit creates qualified leads — companies that have already quantified their AI overspend and are motivated to fix it.

Two monetization paths:

**Path A — Consulting/Optimization Service**
Charge companies a % of the savings identified (e.g., 20% of first-year savings) to actually implement the recommendations (renegotiating contracts, consolidating tools, setting up usage monitoring).

**Path B — SaaS Subscription**
Charge for ongoing monitoring: "We'll alert you when your AI spend drifts above your optimized baseline." $99–$499/month depending on team size.

---

## Path A Math (Consulting)

**Assumptions:**

- Average audit identifies $800/month in savings ($9,600/year)
- Conversion rate from audit → paid engagement: 3%
- Average deal value: 20% of first-year savings = $1,920
- CAC: ~$50 (mostly time cost of community posts + tool hosting)

**To reach $1M ARR:**

$1,000,000 / $1,920 per deal = **521 deals/year** = ~10 deals/month

At 3% conversion from audit to deal:
10 deals/month requires **333 audits/month**

At 60% of visitors running an audit:
333 audits requires **555 visitors/month**

555 visitors/month is very achievable with consistent content distribution.

**LTV/CAC:**

- LTV (single engagement): $1,920
- CAC: $50
- LTV/CAC: **38x** — extremely healthy

---

## Path B Math (SaaS)

**Assumptions:**

- Average contract: $199/month
- Churn: 5%/month (annual churn ~46% — typical for SMB SaaS)
- LTV: $199 / 0.05 = $3,980
- CAC: $150 (paid content + tool hosting + sales time)

**To reach $1M ARR:**

$1,000,000 / ($199 × 12) = **419 customers**

At 1% conversion from audit to paid subscription:
419 customers requires **41,900 audits run**

This requires significant scale — Path B is a year 2+ play after the lead base is established.

---

## Recommended Approach

Start with Path A (consulting) to generate revenue and learn what customers actually need. Use that revenue and customer insight to build Path B (SaaS monitoring) as the scalable product.

The audit tool itself is the top-of-funnel for both paths. Every audit run is a qualified lead with a known dollar value attached.
