## FAQ

**1. Do I need to create an account?**
No. You can run a full audit and see your results without signing up. We only ask for an email if you want to save or share your report.

**2. How accurate is the audit?**
The savings math is deterministic — your spend vs. current published pricing, checked against actual plan limits and team size. We cite every price source. The AI summary is clearly labeled as a summary, not the source of truth.

**3. Where does my data go?**
Your audit is saved to generate a shareable link. We don't sell it, share it with third parties, or use it to train models. If you don't create an account, the report is only accessible via the unique URL.

**4. What tools does it cover?**
Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, Google Gemini, and Windsurf. More on the way.

**5. What if the audit says I'm spending optimally?**
We'll tell you that honestly. "You're spending well" is a valid result. You can sign up for alerts if new optimizations become available as pricing changes.
